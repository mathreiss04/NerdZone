On the eve of the release of "Far Cry 4" , which hits stores on November 18, Ubisoft revealed details about PvP multiplayer mode game, called "Battles of Kyrat."

The battles take place in a time before the events of the campaign "Far Cry 4" , and will have a hand in the villain's bodyguard Pagan Min, the Rakshasa. On the other side are the rebels of the Golden Path.

The Rakshasa use only traditional weapons such as the bow, and are endowed with powers special, and have control over the beasts of Kyrat. Already the Golden Path bases its forces in a powerful arsenal of firearms, as well as have access to many vehicles and technological developments.

According to Ubisoft, the games will be played by two teams of up to five players and should have an average duration of 20 minutes. 10 maps will be available at launch in three distinct modes of competition:. Outpost, Propaganda and Demon Mask.
