Even with a few technical problems, "The Evil Within" is consolidated as a modern classic of horror games and rightful heir to the legacy of "Resident Evil 4".

With surprises and novelties at all times and disturbing and challenging monsters "Evil Within" maintains the atmosphere of suspense and tension all the time, without falling into repetition. The high level of difficulty excites without frustrating too, since the hero Sebastian Castellanos skills and have good enough equipment to overcome challenges along the way.

The controls could be better? Yes, quite, but mastering them turns into more challenging than a hindrance.

For some, the story can be a problem also for being somewhat disjointed and slow to give some explanations, but it also ends up being part of the unique charisma of a bold production Shinji Mikami.

Claudio Prandoni | 10/29/2014 | jogos.uol.com.br
