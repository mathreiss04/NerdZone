Old games for sale on auction sites are nothing new, but the collection of the seller "real big fish" for sale on eBay draws attention: there are 50 islands and 5,700 games, offered at a price of US $ 164 thousand, value equivalent to R $ 400 thousand.

The collection includes almost all games released for Nintendo consoles between 1995 and 2000, and gaming consoles Sega, Atari, Playstation, Xbox and Turbografx. According to the seller, all color variations already released for the Nintendo 64 is part of the collection.

The seller does not intend to dispose of the collection gradually, so, those interested should buy the lot whole. A full list of games and consoles in the collection of "real big fish" can be seen that link. Because of the size and volume of the collection, the seller does not accept offers from outside the United States.
